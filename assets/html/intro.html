About me

I’m a product designer who cares deeply about how things feel in motion. Good design is not only about layout or visual polish. It's about rhythm, feedback, timing, and the small interactions that make an experience intuitive without taking unecessary attention.

I think it is important to refine transitions and micro interactions. They help guide attention, clarify what is happening, and make complex systems easier to understand. When those details are considered, the experience feels more coherent and deliberate.
