Föreningen Stockholms Kendo Allians

Logotype • Website • Graphics • 2015–present

FSKA is a Japanese weapons arts club located in Stockholm, Sweden, that focuses on four diciplines: kendo, iaido, jodo, nagianta. I've been a member since 2002 and have been working with its online presence and marketing.

Logotype

When I joined FSKA, there was already a logotype designed by a former member. It depicted two kendo practitioners (kendoka) facing each other with crossed bamboo swords (shinai). I felt it was time for a refresh. Drawing inspiration from Japanese family crests (kamon), I framed the main graphic inside a circle and removed the lettering. The result feels more modern while still honoring the original design.

Website

In 2014, I took on the task of updating the website. After a few iterations in WordPress with custom CSS, I landed on the above-the-fold hero section: a carousel showcasing the different weapon arts, followed by the main content in the scroll.
