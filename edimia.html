Edimia Education

Logotypes • Product Design • Marketing • 2013–2018

We were a small startup in digital publishing, specializing in learning materials for children. As Head of Graphics and Design, I was responsible for nearly every pixel across both product and marketing.

Logotype

Since the name “Edimia” already stands out in the market, I chose to develop a wordmark logo to highlight its uniqueness. It’s based on a geometric, rounded sans-serif, with a playful tail on the “d” that subtly separates the first two letters from the rest — emphasizing “ed” for education while also hinting at the shape of a speech bubble.

Product Design

We developed two main mathematics titles for students up to third grade. One was a web app optimized for desktop and tablet called Upptäck Mera Matte, and the other was a mobile learning game called Mattemonster. Both products focused on making mathematics engaging, interactive, and visually inviting for young learners.

Marketing

Our marketing spanned both digital and print, targeted toward schools, teachers, and parents. I worked to create cohesive visual materials that ensured the brand felt consistent, approachable, and playful across every touchpoint.
