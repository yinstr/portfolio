A Casino Lobby

Figma • Prototyping • POC • 2025

For this proof-of-concept, I wanted to explore something beyond traditional online casino lobbies we have today. Even with personalization, a long scroll could make it difficult to find content.

I replaced the typical hero banner and game tile grid with vertically swipable cards. Each card has curated content, starting with a brand logo and signup call to action. Swiping up transitions into a top navigation that blends naturally into the experience rather than changing completely, keeping the flow smooth and seamless.

In Figma I tested how the logo, navigation, and content could move fluidly between cards, helping me refine timing and feel.

I also added a full-screen claim offer with a playful and unconventional "prize wheel".

The card layout makes content easier to consume and gives a clear sense of progress, while keeping interactions engaging and the page visually clean, but may feel clumsy when most people are used to conventional scrolling.
