LeoJackpot

LeoVegas • UX/UI • Branding • Animations • 2019

UX/UI

LeoJackpot was one of the first projects I worked on at LeoVegas. It was a third party jackpot solution (now in-house) integrated into selected slot games and live casino tables. Players can choose to join the jackpot and add a small extra amount to each wager for a chance to win one of four jackpot levels: Mini, Minor, Major, or Mega. Together with another UX designer, we designed, prototyped, and tested the user flows and interface.

Branding and Animations

The LeoVegas lion is the core of the LeoJackpot brand. I worked on the logo, jackpot wheel, win animations, and jackpot bar, all built as animated SVGs, later implements as Lottie animations for both web and native applications. The visual style was inspired by the classic diamond shaped Las Vegas sign, updated with clean vector outlines. The same visual language carries through the win animations and logo to create a consistent look and feel.
